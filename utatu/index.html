About Utatu Medicals and Maternity Centre

UTATU MEDICALS AND MATERNITY CENTRE (LLC) is a private, community oriented enterprise owned by a team of experienced and dedicated medical professionals aspiring to provide high quality evidence based curative and preventive medical and maternity services with specialized care in Northern Uganda and parts of South Sudan. Its Adjumani medical and maternity centers is located along Theresa lane, Karai Road, Adjumani town council.

Utatu Medicals and Maternity centre is looking for highly self motivated, innovative and intelligent personalities who are hardworking, dedicated, and smart to join our young, growing and vibrant team to work towards achieving the company vision. The ideal candidate should have the zeal of working with a start ups, and should be mature with high level of emotional intelligence.

We welcome applications from all ethnics and social backgrounds who are willing to work with our diverse teams. We value and regard our staff very highly as an equal opportunity entity we do not discriminate in relation to gender, race, religion, ethnic, and social background.

Our Services

Comprehensive Healthcare Services for Your Entire Family

General Outpatient Consultation

Professional medical consultation for acute and chronic health concerns. Experienced doctors available for diagnosis and treatment.

Maternal & Child Health

Antenatal care, safe delivery services with skilled attendants, postnatal care, and baby care services for the health of mother and child.

Family Planning Services

Comprehensive family planning counseling and services to help families make informed decisions about reproduction.

Immunization & Vaccination

Complete immunization programs for children and vaccination services for adults. CDC-approved vaccines for disease prevention.

Laboratory Services

Full blood tests, diagnostics, and laboratory analysis to support accurate diagnosis and treatment decisions.

Pharmacy & Dispensary

Well-stocked pharmacy with quality medicines at affordable prices. Professional pharmacy staff for medication guidance.

Minor Surgical Procedures

Safe minor surgical procedures including wound care, abscess drainage, and basic surgical interventions.

Emergency & First Aid

24/7 emergency services and first aid care for acute medical and surgical emergencies.

HIV Testing & Care

Confidential HIV testing, counseling services, and management for those affected by HIV/AIDS.

Chronic Disease Management

Management of chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and other long-term health issues with regular monitoring.

Rehabilitation Services

Physiotherapy, rehabilitation exercises, and basic care to restore function and improve quality of life.

Health Education & Prevention

Community health education programs and preventive care strategies to promote wellness and disease prevention.

Maternal & Child Health Excellence

State-of-the-art delivery theatre facility

Safe Delivery Services

Modern delivery facilities with proper hygiene standards and emergency protocols to ensure safe childbirth.

Skilled Birth Attendants

Experienced and trained midwives and doctors dedicated to safe and compassionate childbirth care.

Prenatal Care

Comprehensive antenatal care including monitoring, nutrition counseling, and preparation for childbirth.

Baby Care Services

Newborn care, immunization, and infant health monitoring to ensure healthy development.

Postnatal Care

Follow-up care for mothers and babies after delivery, including health checks and support.

24/7 Availability

Always ready to handle maternal emergencies and provide support whenever you need us.

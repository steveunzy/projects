About Light International

Light International is a child-centered non-profit organization in Uganda (West Nile) dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children through education, health, nutrition, and child protection programs. We partner with communities, schools, health facilities, and government structures to support underserved children.

Vision

A world where every intended child has access to quality education, holistic health, safety, and a future filled with opportunity and dignity.

Mission

To empower vulnerable children through quality education, accessible health services, and protective interventions, while creating supportive communities that nurture growth, resilience, and long-term well-being.

Core Values

  • Compassion – We care deeply for each child we serve.
  • Integrity – We operate with transparency and responsibility.
  • Child Protection – We prioritize safety, dignity, and rights.
  • Excellence – We deliver high-quality, impactful programs.
  • Equity – Every child deserves equal access to opportunity.
  • Partnership – We collaborate with families, communities, and institutions.
  • Innovation – We use creative approaches to solve child-related challenges.

Strategic Objectives

  1. Improve access to quality education for vulnerable children.
  2. Strengthen child health systems and promote healthy development.
  3. Provide protection, psychosocial support, and safe environments for at-risk children.
  4. Build strong community structures for child rights advocacy.
  5. Implement transparent governance, monitoring, and reporting systems.
  6. Develop partnerships with local governments, donors, and stakeholders.

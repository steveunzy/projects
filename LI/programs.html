Our Programs

Education

We promote inclusive, high-quality learning opportunities by:

  • Supporting early childhood education
  • Providing school materials and scholarships
  • Training teachers and caregivers
  • Running community learning centers
  • Supporting digital learning initiatives

Expected Impact: Improved literacy, numeracy, and long-term educational success.

Child Health & Well-being

We work to ensure children receive essential medical care by:

  • Running community health outreach programs
  • Supporting nutrition and feeding programs
  • Providing medical screenings and referrals
  • Promoting mental health and psychosocial support
  • Training caregivers in child health and hygiene

Expected Impact: Healthier, stronger, and more resilient children.

Vulnerable Children Protection

We protect children exposed to risks such as poverty, neglect, abuse, and exploitation by:

  • Establishing safe spaces for children
  • Strengthening child safeguarding systems
  • Supporting orphans and child-headed households
  • Providing emergency assistance
  • Advocating for child rights

Expected Impact: Safer environments and reduced vulnerability.

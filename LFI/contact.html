Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

We maintain transparency in our finances. Typically, 80% goes directly to programs, with 20% for administration and operations. Detailed financial reports are available upon request.

Is Live Free Initiative registered as an NGO?

We are registered with Adjumani District Local Government as a Community-Based Organization. We are currently pursuing registration with the Uganda NGO Bureau and other national bodies.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We welcome remote volunteers for content creation, translation, social media management, and online mentoring. Contact us to discuss opportunities.

What areas does your organization serve?

Currently operating in Adjumani District with plans to expand to Moyo, Yumbe, and Obongi districts. We also engage with refugee populations in the region.

How do I start a Live Free Club in my school?

Contact us with information about your school. We'll provide training, materials, and ongoing support for club leaders. Schools typically see results within a few months.

Can I receive tax benefits for my donation?

Our registration status continues to evolve. During implementation of national registration, consult your accountant about documentation we can provide.

Who We Are

Live Free Initiative is a youth-founded, Christian community-based organization working in Adjumani District to address social challenges through health, education, and livelihood programs.

We believe young people are agents of change. Our mission is to provide vital knowledge and skills that drive personal and community transformation in drug-free spaces where talent and creativity flourish.
